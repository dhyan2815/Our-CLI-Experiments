## Comparison with 2026 AI/ML Trends

### 1. RAG (Retrieval-Augmented Generation)
- **Trend:** Companies want production-grade RAG with hybrid search and multimodal support.
- **Integration Opportunity:** 
    - **AuraOne:** Transform the "Aura Assistant" from simple prompts to a RAG system that searches through the user's notes and tasks to provide contextual answers.
    - **Yield Metrics:** Add a "Farmer's Advisor" RAG feature that uses the predicted yield to search for relevant agricultural practices, crop insurance policies, or market trends.

### 2. Fine-tuning
- **Trend:** Domain-specific "Sovereign AI" and fine-tuning for personality/expertise.
- **Integration Opportunity:**
    - **SmokeSignal-AI:** Fine-tune a small model (e.g., Phi-3-mini) on wildfire prevention manuals and historical data to provide expert-level advice alongside the detection system.
    - **AuraOne:** Fine-tune a model to match the user's personal productivity style and "voice" for better note summarization and task prioritization.

### 3. Agentic AI & MLOps
- **Trend:** Shift from chatbots to autonomous agents and full-lifecycle MLOps.
- **Integration Opportunity:**
    - **Agent-Sandbox:** This should be your flagship "Agentic AI" project. Build a multi-agent system where agents collaborate on complex tasks (e.g., one agent researches, another writes, a third verifies).
    - **SmokeSignal-AI / Yield Metrics:** Implement a full MLOps pipeline (MLflow, Docker, monitoring) for these existing models to show production readiness.
