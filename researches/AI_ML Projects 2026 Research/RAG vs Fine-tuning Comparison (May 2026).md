# RAG vs Fine-tuning Comparison (May 2026)

| Dimension | RAG | Fine-tuning |
|-----------|-----|-------------|
| Setup cost | $500–$5,000 | $50–$20,000+ |
| Monthly operating cost | $500–$15,000 | Lower per-query (no retrieval overhead) |
| Time to production | 2–4 weeks | 4–12 weeks |
| Data freshness | Real-time | Frozen at training time |
| Best for | Dynamic knowledge, frequent updates | High-volume, stable, latency-critical tasks |

## Key Insights for 2026
- RAG is the default for most enterprise use cases (85%).
- Fine-tuning is preferred for high-volume, stable-knowledge use cases.
- RAG year-one cost is roughly 60% of fine-tuning ($18,400 vs $30,600 for typical customer support).
- MLOps and monitoring are critical as 80%+ of AI projects fail due to poor data prep, integration, and maintenance.
- Agentic AI is an emerging trend, multiplying project costs by 2-4x.
